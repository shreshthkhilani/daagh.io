about


Daagh means stain in Urdu. We enable artists to leave their marks — their stains — on the world.

We make websites for individuals and organizations.

We can also advise social media strategies, grant applications, and production management. We care tremendously about the arts in Philadelphia and want to serve the community by helping our peers present their work.
